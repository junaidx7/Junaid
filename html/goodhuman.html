Nathan Digital Accounting" is a cutting-edge accounting software that revolutionises accounting for businesses of all sizes. With its intuitive interface and powerful features, Nathan Digital Accounting simplifies complex tasks, allowing users to effortlessly track transactions, manage accounts receivable and accounts payable, and generate comprehensive financial reports. The software seamlessly integrates with Nathan digital's other products to make a system that is extremely simple & convenient to use.

MY ROLE ; As a sole Designer

As the sole designer for the NathanDigital Accounting software , I worked closely with stakeholders, and in-house accountants to understand the core requirement of the software and develop a design that was both user-friendly, intuitive efficient. I also collaborated closely with developers to ensure that my designs were feasible and implementable.

  • Conducted user research to understand the needs and pain points
  • Developed a design system to ensure a consistent look and feel for the software
  • Created wireframes and prototypes
  • Conducted usability testing & performed required iterations
  • Collaborated with developers to implement the design
  • Conducted usability testing to ensure that the design was user-friendly and efficient

Setting the Goal.

The bar was set high; we aimed to compete with already-established industry giants such as QuickBooks and Xero. For this purpose, we sought to create software that not only accomplished user’s tasks efficiently but also proved to be more intuitive, user-friendly, powerful, and capable of seamless integration with Nathan-digital’s other ERPs.

  • Intuitive task execution - Should be able to perform common task such as creating invoice & sharing invoice, Adding expense, running reports, and other tasks faster and in a more intuitive way.
  • Don’t make the user think - Reduce cognitive load of the user as much as possible, for eg, when ever the system knows the data, it should auto populate the fields rather than user filling in the data.
  • Don’t make user do it twice - Introduce features that automate repetitive tasks, saving users time and effort in their accounting processes.
  • ERP integration: The software should integrate with companies' ERP products, providing a seamless workflow and eliminating the need to switch between multiple systems.
  • Visually Appealing - The Interface should be visually appealing and must have a dark mode to reduce eye strain.

By learning from their approaches, I created a user-friendly and feature-rich design that sets Nathan Digital Accounting apart. This thorough analysis ensured our software not only competes but excels in the competitive landscape, offering users a superior accounting solution.

Information
Architecture

when designing Nathan Digital Accounting, meticulous attention was given to information architecture, the backbone of a seamless user experience. By strategically organizing complex financial data, categorizing transactions, and structuring the navigation flow, I ensured that users could effortlessly comprehend and interact with the software. Clear categorization and intuitive labeling empower users to swiftly locate crucial information, fostering a sense of control and confidence in their financial management. The thoughtful information architecture not only simplifies complex accounting processes but also elevates the overall usability, making Nathan Digital Accounting an intuitive and user-friendly solution for businesses of all scales.
